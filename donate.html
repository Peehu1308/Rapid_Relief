Frequently Asked Questions

How is my donation used?
Your donation directly supports our disaster relief and rehabilitation efforts. Funds are allocated to emergency response, medical care, food and water distribution, temporary shelter, and long-term rehabilitation projects based on the most urgent needs. We maintain a transparent system where at least 85% of all donations go directly to program implementation.
Is my donation tax-deductible?
Yes, all donations to RapidRelief are tax-deductible under Section 80G of the Income Tax Act in India. You will receive a donation receipt that can be used for tax purposes.
Can I specify which disaster my donation helps?
Yes, you can specify a particular disaster or project during the donation process. However, unrestricted funds allow us to respond most effectively to emerging crises and direct resources where they are needed most urgently.
How do I know my donation is making an impact?
We provide regular updates through our newsletter, social media channels, and annual impact reports. These communications detail our relief operations, beneficiary stories, and the specific outcomes achieved through donor support. You can also visit our Projects page to see our ongoing and completed initiatives.
Can I make a recurring donation?
Yes, we offer monthly, quarterly, and annual recurring donation options. Recurring donations provide us with sustainable funding to plan long-term interventions and maintain readiness for emergency response.
